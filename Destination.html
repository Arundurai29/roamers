Itinerary

  • Hey, wlcome to the land of coconuts! Meet our Roamers team and captains at Kochi as our exciting journey starts @11 am to the most beloved hill station; Vagamon.
  • It's going to be about 3-hour Journey. During the drive, let's share stories, laughter, and get to know each other while enjoying the mesmerizing views of the Vagamon hills.
  • Upon reaching Vagamon, settle in your hotel and enjoy the ambience. Let's head out to explore the local market at eve and indulge in a delightful local-style dinner.
  • Get ready for an electrifying night on the hilltop! let's danceand sing under the stars, creating unforgettable memories and endless laughter.
  • Rest and recharge for an exciting day ahead, as our Vagamon adventure has just begun.

  • Let's start our day bright and early, heading straight to the Vagamon Meadows. Explore the serene landscapes, Take leisure strolls, snap stunning photos and soak in the tranquil atmosphere.
  • Alright, it's time to fly Roamers! We'll head to the paragliding activity spot. Experience the thrill of paragliding as you glide through the clouds and witness the breathtaking aerial views of Vagamon's mesmerizing landscape. Those who aren't into sport activities, no worries! just relax and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere.
  • Next, let's visit the renowned Pine Forest. Take your time to wander and soak in the serene ambiance, surrounded by majestic pine trees.
  • Post lunch, let's head towards Marmala Waterfalls for a trekking adventure. The trek to the waterfalls offers us the opportunity to explore the untouched beauty of the surroundings, navigate through rugged terrain, and get closer to the breathtaking Marmala Waterfall
  • Wrap up the day with a delightful dinner and relax in your cozy accommodation.

  • Post breakfast, bid farewell to the hills as we embark on our journey towards the coastal town of Varkala.
  • Enroute we'll stop at the fascinating Jatayu Earth Centre. Discover the larger-than-life sculpture of Jatayu, the mythical bird from the Indian epic Ramayana, which stands as a symbol of courage and valor. Get to know the history and stories from Team Captians
  • We'll take a cable car ride to the top of the center and you'll be rewarded with breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes, don't forget to capture stunning photos here gang!
  • Then, we'll check in to our hotel at varkala and Roamers, feel free to have a 'Me Time' roam to near by beach, cliffs. Explore the vibrant local markets to find unique souvenirs for your loved ones and indulge in the flavors of Varkala by visiting popular cafes and treating yourself.
  • Afterwards, we'll make our way back to our cozy hotel to unwind and recharge. Get ready for an exhilarating adventure that awaits us tomorrow.

  • Start the day with a surfing lesson at Kappil Beach, which is known for its consistent waves and surf-friendly conditions. If you're not into surfing, no worries! immerse in the beach vibe and rest on the nature's lap.
  • Afterwards, let's head to check the famous varkala beach, Black Beach and Papanasam Beach.
  • Post Lunch, We're going to the Mangroove forest to spill our adventure spirits. Indulge in kayaking , Cycling, stand up paddling or trekking to explore local rituals, cuisines and lifestyles spending the entire eve.
  • After satisfying day, let's head to our hotel for a good night sleep.

  • Post breakast, Say goodbye to Varkala. We'll checkout from our hotel and driving to Alleppey which is touted as the 'Backwater capital of India'.
  • Check into our floating paradise, the houseboat, and indulge in a delectable local lunch while cruising along the mesmerizing backwaters. Immerse yourself in the tranquility as we glide through the picturesque Vembanad Lake and Punnamada Lake.
  • But the excitement doesn't stop thereyou can hop on a thrilling speed boat ride, exploring the charming villages and hidden gems that surround us. Capture the sunsets while having kerala traditional tea & Snack
  • Let's give a thought about poor souls who left kerala just visiting one place and left. Finally, we'll retire for the night with a traditional Kerala-style dinner, music, and gang in the middle of the sea.

  • After Breakfast on houseboat, let's checkout and begin day by visiting the Alappuzha Beach, where you can take a stroll along the shoreline and enjoy the serene coastal ambiance.
  • Afternoon, we'll take a boat ride to Pathiramanal, a beautiful island in the Vembanad Lake known for its scenic beauty and bird watching opportunities. Spend time exploring the island's natural wonders and spotting various bird species.
  • Enjoy a delicious seafood lunch at a local restaurant, savoring the flavors of the region's culinary delights.
  • At eve, we'll head to the Punnamada Lake, find a serene spot along the shoreline, and witness a mesmerizing sunset over the backwaters, creating a picturesque view.
  • We came to an end of our Journey Roamers! We will give this trip a fitting finale by hosting an all-inclusive farewell dinner to make it truly special. Let's reflect and see how the strangers has become family now.

  • After breakfast, we'll depart from Alleppey and drive back to Kochi.
  • Goodbyes are never easy, but what a ride it has been! In just 7 days, we shared thrills, spills, and countless good times, let's plan a reunion trip next year to continue the adventure. 100% guaranteed!
  • See you guys, more Hugs!
